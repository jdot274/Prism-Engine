Notes
-----
* "Forked Lyra" assumes the architecture in research/threejs-ue5-hybrid/05-ue5-
  physics-only-architecture.md sec. 5.9 (rendering/HUD/MetaHuman stripped;
  Experiences + GAS + EOS retained).
* The bidirectional "scene.v1 JSON" stream feeds both runtimes from the same
  compiled source; see 07-meta-scene-tool-design.md for the DSL spec.
* Optional Pixel Streaming 2 edge is the path described in 04-fake-rendering-
  tricks.md sec. 4.1 -- specifically the "creative" usage with a Three.js HTML
  overlay over the streamed video.
* The total custom-build budget for the bespoke pieces (UExporterSubsystem,
  Rust gateway, SWC transform, R3F morphScene reconciler, UE5 hot-reload
  plugin) is approximately 3-4 months of one engineer's focused work; see
  10-build-vs-buy.md for the breakdown.
